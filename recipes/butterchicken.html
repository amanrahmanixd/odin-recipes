Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a tomato-based gravy.

It is known for its buttery texture, mild spices, and is best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt (for marination)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/4 cup cashew paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook or grill the marinated chicken until tender and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Mix in cashew paste, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked chicken to the gravy and mix well.
  7. Pour in fresh cream and crushed kasuri methi. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and a swirl of cream.
  9. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
