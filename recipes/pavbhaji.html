Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with butter-toasted bread rolls.

It's a popular Indian street food known for its rich flavors and vibrant taste.

Here are the main ingredients for Pav Bhaji:

Vegetables:

  • Potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • Cauliflower
  • Green peas
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies (optional)

Spices & Flavor:

  • Pav Bhaji masala
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Boil and mash the vegetables: potatoes, cauliflower, peas, and capsicum.
  2. Heat butter in a large pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and pav bhaji masala; mix well.
  6. Add the mashed vegetables and mix thoroughly with the masala.
  7. Use a masher to blend everything together while cooking.
  8. Add water as needed to adjust consistency and simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  9. Add a knob of butter and garnish with chopped coriander and lemon juice.
  10. Toast pav buns on a pan with butter until golden and crisp.
  11. Serve hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.
