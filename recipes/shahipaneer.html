Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a royal North Indian dish made with paneer cooked in a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced gravy.

It’s often prepared with cashews, cream, and aromatic spices, making it perfect for festive meals.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Soak cashews in warm water for 15 minutes and blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cashew paste and cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring continuously.
  7. Add paneer cubes and gently mix them into the gravy.
  8. Pour in fresh cream and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
